DIY Photoshop

DIY Photoshop - p5.js - 2024

I made a DIY Photoshop that allows you to create graffiti. It has a brick wall background and tools that are thin and thicker thicknesses of black, yellow, blue, and red, in addition to shading and star shaped highlights.

Keybinds:

1 - Thin Black

2 - Thick Black

3 - Thin Yellow

4 - Thick Yellow

5 - Thin Blue

6 - Thick Blue

7 - Thin Red

8 - Thick Red

9 - Black Shading

0 - Star Highlight

X - Clear Canvas

P - Screenshot
