Graffiti PS - 2024

p5.js

Graffiti PS is a digital graffiti tool that allows you to graffiti whatever you desire with no consequences. This work allows the user to explore their urges and impulses in a digital environment.

Keybinds:
1 - Thin Black
2 - Thick Black
3 - Thin Yellow
4 - Thick Yellow
5 - Thin Blue
6 - Thick Blue
7 - Thin Red
8 - Thick Red
9 - Black Shading
0 - Star Highlight
X - Clear Canvas
P - Screenshot
